I am Christian Isorena, a 20-year-old IT student at National University Fairview, currently residing with my parents in Amparo, Caloocan City. As the youngest member of my family, I've always had a keen interest in mobile gaming since I was a child. Perhaps it's because I was born in this era of automation. Despite having the opportunity to play outdoors, mobile gaming has remained my favorite pastime. Even now, my phone serves as my trusted companion. Mobile Legends stands out as one of my preferred gaming applications. I enjoy the adrenaline rush and sense of accomplishment it offers. Whether I win or lose, the excitement it brings is truly satisfying.
